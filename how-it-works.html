How Spritico works

Three steps from messy handwriting to clean, exportable digital notes — all inside your browser, nothing uploaded to any server.

1

Step one

Upload your note

Drag and drop or click to upload any photo, scan, or PDF of your handwritten or typed notes. Spritico accepts JPG, PNG, WEBP and PDF files. Even low-quality photos taken under bad lighting work fine — the image enhancer sharpens and binarises the image before reading it.

✓ JPG  ·  PNG  ·  WEBP  ·  PDF
2

Step two

Spritico reads and structures it

The image is enhanced right in your browser — scaled up, contrast-stretched, sharpened, and binarised with an adaptive threshold. The OCR engine then reads every character and the formatter intelligently detects headings, bullet points, numbered lists, formulas, and labels — turning raw text into clean structured notes.

✓ Runs 100% in your browser — nothing sent to any server
3

Step three

Export in any format

Once your notes are digitised, copy them to clipboard or download as a polished PDF, a Word document (.doc), or a plain text file (.txt). All exports are formatted cleanly — the PDF includes proper heading styles, the Word doc is ready to edit, and the .txt is clean UTF-8.

✓ PDF  ·  Word  ·  .txt  ·  Copy

Frequently asked questions

Is my data private? Does anything get uploaded?

Completely private. Spritico runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your images and notes never leave your device — there is no backend server, no database, and nothing is stored anywhere.

What languages does Spritico support?

Currently English is fully supported. The OCR engine reads Latin-script characters reliably. Support for Hindi, Arabic, and other scripts is planned for a future update.

My handwriting is very messy — will it still work?

Spritico pre-processes every image to maximise OCR accuracy — it scales up, sharpens, and binarises your image before reading it. Very neat handwriting gets near-perfect results. Very messy or cursive handwriting may have some errors, but the output will always be structured and clean. Taking the photo in good lighting helps significantly.

Can I convert multi-page PDFs?

Yes. Spritico processes each page of your PDF in order and combines them into one clean output with page separators. Large PDFs may take a little longer since everything runs in your browser.

Is Spritico free?

Yes — completely free, no account required, no usage limits. Convert as many notes as you like.
